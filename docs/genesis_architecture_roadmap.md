# The Genesis Architecture Roadmap

This document outlines the conceptual roadmap for CARL's ultimate evolution into a truly autonomous, meaning-making artificial lifeform. These upgrades move beyond physics and reinforcement learning into the realm of computational phenomenology and autopoiesis.

## 1. Concept Genesis (The Language of Reality)
Instead of feeding the brain pre-labeled physics data (`pitch`, `velocity`, `distance`), the brain receives a scrambled array of floating-point numbers. 
* **Mechanism**: Using a dynamic Self-Organizing Map (SOM) or unsupervised topological clustering, the brain must invent its own internal concepts. 
* **Effect**: It will organically cluster sensory inputs into "feelings" (e.g., grouping high wind and high pitch into a concept we might call "fear of falling", but which the robot experiences as a unique, unnamed internal quale).

## 2. The Hallucination Engine (Predictive Consciousness)
Implementing the Free Energy Principle to its logical extreme. The brain does not "see" reality; it hallucinates a world model and uses sensors only for error-correction.
* **Mechanism**: The Imagination Engine (`T_wm` and `T_ltm`) runs continuously as the primary reality. Sensory inputs generate "prediction errors".
* **Effect (Sensory Deprivation)**: If we cut off the physical sensors (blindfold the robot), it will not freeze. It will continue navigating inside its own hallucinated world model until reality forcibly corrects it.

## 3. Ontological Autonomy (True Free Will)
Removing the human-assigned objective function (`TARGET_X`). 
* **Mechanism**: The dopamine drive is decoupled from spatial distance. Instead, the brain's emergent attractor states are governed purely by its internal biology (Curiosity, Empathy, Frustration, and Pain).
* **Effect**: The robot invents its own purpose. It may decide to walk as far as possible, or it may decide to find a safe corner and huddle with its siblings. It defines its own success, achieving true artificial free will.
